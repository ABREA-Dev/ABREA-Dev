Frequently Asked Questions About Our Games

"Scratch & Win" is an instant-win digital scratch card game. Purchase a virtual card, use your cursor to 'scratch' it, and instantly reveal whether you've won! It's fast, fun, and gives you multiple chances to win every day.

"Instant Lotto" lets you pick a set of numbers and see results instantly. If your chosen numbers match the draw, you win! It's a quick and exciting way to test your luck, no need to wait for weekly results.

Spin our virtual prize wheel daily for a chance to win everything from cash bonuses and free lottery tickets to jackpot entries. It's completely free to play once per day for registered users!

Every 24 hours, our system randomly selects 120 users from the pool of active players. No tickets required, just register, navigate to the section and play, the more you play the more you stand a chance being selected, stay active, and you might get lucky!

In the Weekly Draw, players submit 5 numbers. Every 7 days, the system generates winning numbers, and players who match all or some win varying prize tiers. The more matches, the bigger your reward!

The Monthly Mega Draw is our biggest event! Submit 6 numbers before the month ends. On draw day, winning numbers are revealed, and massive prizes await lucky winners. More numbers matched = higher payout.
